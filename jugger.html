International Jugger Council

Jugger—a blend of capture the flag, fencing, and rugby—enjoys relative popularity in Germany, but my partner and I introduced the game to the Bay Area and Toronto. I designed our logos and uniforms, along with several giveaways for players and other teams we met at tournaments. I created an Instructable, later featured, to document our building process. My partner and I gave a presentation at Nerd Nite East Bay on the history of the sport, for which I crafted the slides. I am also on the board of the International Jugger Council and designed their website.
