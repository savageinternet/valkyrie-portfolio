The Puzzling Madness of John A. Macdonald is a puzzle/scavenger hunt across Downtown Toronto. Designed for the Canada 150 celebration, it features puzzles set in Toronto of 1867, using historical handbills, maps, and sites. I am the art director and designer for the project, photographing and creating ad campaigns, crafting print and physical props, costuming actors, co-supervising auditions and rehearsals, and running game experience tests.
