Proximity Dress
sewn electronics
github repository

For our wedding, my husband and I also crafted a pair of proximity-monitoring electronic devices. In his pocket was a transmitter, and my dress held a receiver. Based on distance between the radios, the dress would also light up more or less. This experience was a big hit for our guests.
