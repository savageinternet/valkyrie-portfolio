Zombie Rendezvous

blog post

Zombie Rendezvous is designed for play in in 30 minutes or less by children ages 4-12. Players receive a paper game booklet when they are infected by a zombie. Through the course of curing their infection, players learn about the difference between bacterial, viral, and fungal infections, how doctors prevent the spread of diseases, how pathologists grow cultures of diseases, and how researchers design drugs. I designed and illustrated the entire booklet, with the exception of the three cover zombies.
