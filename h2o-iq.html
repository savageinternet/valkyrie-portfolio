H2O IQ is a smart watering system with drip irrigation. It includes an in-garden device and a web interface. The device's uses a moisture sensor and a servo to control water flow, as well as a custom on-board microcontroller and XBee radio to update a server's record of its moisture and query for optimal or desired watering settings. I designed the web interface and electronics code.
