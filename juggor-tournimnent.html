Gather room

During the COVID lockdowns of 2020-2021, I was inspired to bring together the community for my sport using video conferencing. I developed a modified ruleset for our sport which is playable over the spatial video-conferencing platform gather. The ruleset was officially debuted on May 1 to a crowd of juggers, and the server time was paid for via a small kickstarter.
