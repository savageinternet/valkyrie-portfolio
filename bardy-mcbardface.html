Bardy McBardface
playable game prototype
personal
design sketch (w/ Evan)
github repository

Intrigued by modern machine language generation, I prototyped a game with poetry as a part of its core game loop. In Bardy McBardface, you play a bard who tries not to die while simultaneously being close enough to a fight between your hero and their enemy to collect (generated) scraps of verse which you later assemble into a ballad.
