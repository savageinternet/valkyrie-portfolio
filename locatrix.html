Locatrix
Android app and website
Savage Internet
web home
on Google Play

Locatrix tracks a user's location and makes it easy to share, while preserving user privacy and safety by only sharing city-level location. Locatrix comes in two parts: an app which is downloaded to the user's phone and a website which leads the user through configuring their sharing tools. I was the sole UI/UX designer for the app and website, and contributed to coding.
